How to Use the Website?

  • Homepage: Click on "Search Hotels" to proceed.
  • Login or Skip: You can log in or use the "Skip" button to continue without logging in.
  • Select a City: Choose from the best cities with images.
  • View Hotels: After selecting a city, you will see all available hotels.
  • Hotel Details: Click on any hotel to view its complete information.
  • Give Feedback: Provide a rating and share your experience.
